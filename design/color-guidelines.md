# Color Guidelines for UI/UX Design

## Purpose
These guidelines help designers choose colors that improve usability, 
accessibility, and user experience.

## Recommended Colors

### Blue (#2563EB)
- Best for login screens
- Banking and finance apps
- Technology products
- Builds trust and security

### Green (#16A34A)
- Success messages
- Confirmation buttons
- Health and finance apps
- Represents growth and positivity

### Red (#DC2626)
- Error messages
- Warning alerts
- Limited-time offers
- Use sparingly to avoid overwhelming users

### Yellow (#FACC15)
- Highlights
- Notifications
- Warning messages
- Avoid large yellow backgrounds

### Orange (#F97316)
- Call-to-action buttons
- Promotions
- Friendly and energetic interfaces

### Purple (#7C3AED)
- Creative platforms
- Luxury brands
- Beauty and entertainment apps

### Black (#111827)
- Headings
- Premium designs
- Main text for readability

### White (#FFFFFF)
- Backgrounds
- Minimal interfaces
- Improves readability and cleanliness

## Accessibility Tips
- Maintain sufficient color contrast.
- Don't rely on color alone to convey information.
- Test designs for color blindness.
- Keep the interface simple and consistent.

## Conclusion
Using colors thoughtfully creates interfaces that are attractive, 
accessible, and easy to use.
