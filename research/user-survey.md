# User Survey

## Objective
The purpose of this survey is to understand how different colors influence users' 
emotions, preferences, and trust while using digital applications.

## Survey Participants
- Total Participants: 20
- Age Group: 18–35 years
- Device Used: Mobile and Laptop

## Survey Questions

### 1. Which color gives you the most trust?
- Blue
- Green
- Black
- White

### 2. Which color grabs your attention first?
- Red
- Yellow
- Orange
- Purple

### 3. Which color makes an app feel calm?
- Blue
- Green
- White
- Purple

### 4. Which color would you prefer for a "Buy Now" button?
- Red
- Green
- Blue
- Orange

### 5. Which color combination is easiest to read?
- Black text on White background
- White text on Black background
- Blue text on White background
- Gray text on White background

## Sample Survey Results

- 60% chose **Blue** as the most trustworthy color.
- 55% said **Red** attracts attention first.
- 70% preferred **Blue or Green** for a calm experience.
- 65% found **Black text on a White background** easiest to read.
- 50% preferred **Orange** for action buttons.

## Conclusion
The survey indicates that users generally associate blue with trust, red with urgency, and green with calmness.
High-contrast color combinations also improve readability and accessibility.
