# Color Theory

## Introduction
Color theory is the study of how colors interact and how they influence human emotions, perceptions, and behavior. In UI/UX design, choosing the right colors improves usability, accessibility, and user satisfaction.

## Primary Colors
- Red
- Blue
- Yellow

These colors cannot be created by mixing other colors.

## Secondary Colors
- Green
- Orange
- Purple

These are formed by mixing two primary colors.

## Warm Colors
Warm colors include:
- Red
- Orange
- Yellow

They create feelings of:
- Energy
- Excitement
- Passion
- Attention

## Cool Colors
Cool colors include:
- Blue
- Green
- Purple

They create feelings of:
- Calmness
- Trust
- Peace
- Professionalism

## Importance in UI/UX
Color plays a major role in:
- Improving readability
- Creating visual hierarchy
- Highlighting important actions
- Building brand identity
- Increasing user engagement

## Conclusion
Understanding color theory helps designers create visually appealing, accessible, and user-friendly interfaces that improve the overall user experience.
